Employee Safety

The Safety of Employees is our number #1 priority. To prove it, Cardinal IG Hood River, has become Oregon OSHA’s 21st Voluntary Protective Programs (VPP) Star Certified workplace. The VPP Star certification recognizes employers who go beyond what is required by their industry safety standards. VPP certified jobsites have up to 80% fewer workday injuries than those of a similar workplace that is not certified, helping to assure our employees they have a safe place to work.

Find Out More: https://www.osha.gov/vpp/all-about-vpp
